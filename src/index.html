ABOUT US

What we stand for

Liberalism

Liberty

America is the land of the free. We believe in the rights of the people to live their own life and choose their own destiny, and a nation that respects their autonomy and listens to their voice.

Equality

Even through all its flaws, America is exceptional. We believe that our nation is and should be that proverbial shining city on a hill. It is one of our greatest strengths that anyone can earn the right to be called an American.

Electoralism

Duty

Freedom isn't free, but voting is. We believe that democracy functions best when everyone makes their voice heard, and voting is our best tool to shape America's future. Voting matters; our strength comes in numbers.

Pragmatism

It is an essential feature of democracy that we can't always get what we want. We believe that fighting for the candidate that aligns most with our ideals, rather than petty infighting, is far more likely to get us where we want to go.
